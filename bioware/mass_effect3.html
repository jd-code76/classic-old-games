Brief History of Mass Effect 3

Originally released on March 6th, 2012 for Windows, Xbox 360, and PlayStation 3, Mass Effect 3 is the final game in the Mass Effect trilogy. Like ME2 before it, ME3 massively improved the gameplay, adding much better cover mechanics, better shooting, more abilities, and more. The game also added a horde style multiplayer mode that was very well received.

ME3 concluded the story of Commander Shepard and his quest to eliminate the Reapers. In the original controversial ending, the game boils the player's choices down to a few button presses, greatly upsetting fans who had invested so much time in the series. This ending was slightly improved with a free DLC.

As a whole, ME3 sold well and was a decent conclusion to the Mass Effect trilogy.
