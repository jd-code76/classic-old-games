Brief History of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic

Originally released on July 15th, 2003 for the original Xbox, KotOR is widely considered to be one of the best games of all time. Based off of the 3rd edition D&D rules, KotOR refined the formula that BioWare created with Baldur's Gate. The game allows a great degree of freedom in player choice for multiple builds, dialogue, and exploration.

The story of KotOR sees the player visiting many planets, some from classic Star Wars fame like Tatooine, but also include new locations like Taris and Manaan. Each location has its own questline that needs to be resolved in order to advance the story. Once the player receives his lightsaber, he is free to visit these planets in any order he chooses.

KotOR received huge critical and commercial success, becoming a fan favorite Star Wars game. To this day, the game retains a dedicated modding community that continues to improve the game's graphics, add new items, fix bugs, and more.
