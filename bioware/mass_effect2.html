Brief History of Mass Effect 2

Originally released on January 26th, 2010 for Windows and Xbox 360, Mass Effect 2 was a massive improvement over the first game. BioWare significantly improved the shooting mechanics, graphics, and improved galaxy exploration. Additionally, BioWare introduced many new characters that expanded the storytelling potential.

ME2 continues Commander Shepard's story, opening with the Normandy under attack. Ultimately, Shepard is "killed" and brought back from the dead and placed under the employ of the Illusive Man. From there, Shepard is sent across the galaxy on a suicide mission to save the galaxy.

ME2 went on to become one of the best sequels and one of the best games of all time, selling millions of copies and receiving multiple DLC packs that added new companions and questlines. The game did receive a PS3 released about a year after the Windows and 360 release. However, the PS3 version contained many bugs, the worst of which included save file corruption.
