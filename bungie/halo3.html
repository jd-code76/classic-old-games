Brief History of Halo 3

Originally released on September 25th, 2007 for the Xbox 360, Halo 3 continued the franchise's tradition by breaking several sales records. The game improved on Halo 2's core gameplay by adding many more weapons, vehicles, and added other brand new features like Forge and Theater. The game was also the first Halo game to include a four-player co-op mode for the campaign.

Like Halo 2, Halo 3 obtained a highly dedicated playerbase and was one of the most played games on the Xbox 360 for the entirety of the console's life cycle.
