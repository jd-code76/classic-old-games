Brief History of Marathon 2: Durandal

Originally released on November 24th, 1995 for the Apple Macintosh, Durandal is the second game in the Marathon Trilogy. Durandal expanded on the Marathon formula by adding more multiplayer modes, increasing the screen view, and adding swimming to the game. In addition, the Durandal engine also allowed for more ambient sounds.

Durandal received an Xbox Live Arcade port for the Xbox 360 on August 1st, 2007. This version of the game included new HD textures, sprites, four player split-screen, and a faster framerate. Notably, this version does not allow the player to save film recordings due to Xbox Live storage limitations.
