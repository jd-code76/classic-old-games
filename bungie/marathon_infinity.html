Brief History of Marathon: Infinity

Originally released on October 15th, 1996 for the Apple Macintosh, Marathon: Infinity is the third and final game in the Marathon Trilogy. Infinity kept all the gameplay elements of Durandal and added a new weapon as well as improving on the mapping and graphics tools.

The story of Infinity takes place in various alternate realities where the player is tasked with completing various objectives throughout the game's 25 level campaign. As with the other Marathon games, Bungie released the source code for Infinity, allowing mappers and modders to greatly extend the life of the game.
