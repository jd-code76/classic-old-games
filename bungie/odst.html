Brief History of Halo 3: ODST

Originally released on September 22nd, 2009 for the Xbox 360, Halo 3: ODST was originally meant to be an expansion for Halo 3. However, as the development continued, Bungie decided to turn ODST into a spin-off game, giving the player a chance to play as an ODST for the first time in the franchise.

While ODST did not include traditional Halo multiplayer, the standard version of the game included a second disc with all the multiplayer maps ever released for Halo 3. Bungie also created a new co-op only mode called "Firefight" that plays similarly to Gears of War's horde mode. Like Halo 3, ODST also includes the ability to play the entire campaign in four player co-op.
