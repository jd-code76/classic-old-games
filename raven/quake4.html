Brief History of Quake 4

Originally released on October 18th, 2005 for Windows, Quake 4 was a collaboration between Raven Software and id Software, with id overseeing production and Raven doing the actual development. Quake 4 is a narrative sequel to Quake II following a Marine who is tasked with securing Stroggos (the Strogg home world). The game contains a multiplayer mode similar to Quake III, but does not include bots without the use of mods. The Xbox 360 version of Quake 4 also contains a playable copy of Quake II on a bonus disc.
