Brief History of Lord of the Rings: Rise of the Witch King

Originally released in November 28th, 2006 by EA Games, Rise of the Witch King is the expansion pack for The Battle for Middle-earth II, developed by EA Los Angeles. The expansion adds a new faction, campaign, and various gameplay improvements.

The game introduces the Angmar faction, led by the Witch-king of Angmar, featuring unique units and mechanics focused on corruption and terror. The campaign explores the rise of Angmar in the Third Age and its war against the kingdom of Arnor.

Rise of the Witch King was well-received for its compelling new faction, challenging campaign, and additional content that expanded the BFME II experience. It added new maps, heroes, and a War of the Ring mode that allowed for more strategic gameplay.
