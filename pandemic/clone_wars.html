Brief History of Star Wars: The Clone Wars

Originally released on October 28th, 2002 for GameCube, Star Wars: The Clone Wars is a console exclusive Star Wars game. It follows the events of the Clone Wars, beginning with the Battle of Geonosis. The primary characters are Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker, and Mace Windu.

The game is unique in that it focuses heavily on vehicle combat, allowing the player to utilize various Republic tanks, gunships, and other classic Star Wars vehicles. The campaign consists of 16 levels across six different planets, each with their own objectives.

Additionally, there are several two player multiplayer modes:

  1. Academy - Fight off endless waves of enemies on foot (Geonosis arena) or in vehicles (Thule moon)
  2. Conquest - Control and defend your base
  3. Control Zone - King of the hill
  4. Duel Mode - 1v1 duels on several planets
