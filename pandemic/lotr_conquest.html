Brief History of The Lord of the Rings: Conquest

Originally released on January 9th, 2009 for Windows, PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, and Nintendo DS, Lord of the Rings: Conquest is an action game built on the same engine Pandemic used for the Battlefront and Mercenaries series. Conquest allows the player to choose from a variety of campaigns and characters, similar to Battlefront.

In terms of gameplay, Conquest is functionally identical to Battlefront, allowing the player to choose a class and capture control points in order to wear down enemy forces in order to win. Using an updated version of the updated Zero engine from Battlefront, Conquest allows for up to 150 units to be on the battlefield at one time.

Additionally, a vastly different version of the game was developed for the Nintendo DS.
