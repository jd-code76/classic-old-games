Brief History of Destroy All Humans! 2

Originally released on October 17th, 2006 for PS2 and Xbox, Destroy All Humans 2 picks up 10 years after the first. Crypto-137 (the clone protagonist from the first game) is deceased and Crypto-138 takes his place as the new player character.

Like the first game, Destroy All Humans 2 contains similar gameplay and plot elements. The player has access to new abilities and weapons, including The Dislocator (shoots discs that levitate targets) and body-snatching (allowing the player to disguise himself as humans). A co-op mode was also added, allowing two players to play the story mode and engage in free roam minigames.

Many players state that the game is superior to its predecessor for its many new features and mechanics. In 2021, a remaster of the game was announced and is expected to release on ninth generation consoles and PC.
